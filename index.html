This is a collection of projects that I made to practice my web development skills. These projects were made by following freeCodeCamp's excellent Certified Full Stack Developer Curriculum. The Workshops section in each chapter contains guided projects that are provided in the curriculum to introduce various html+css concepts to learners. On the other hand, the Labs sections contain projects that I've made to pass freeCodeCamp's rigorous user stories to get through their course.
